Triathlon recap, Aug. 5: Germany surges to gold, U.S. earns silver in team relay

  • Dan Levinsohn
  • 2024-08-15

Trois triathlon events at the 2024 Paris Olympics, trois winning countries: France's Cassandre Beaugrand won the women's individual, Great Britain's Alex Yee broke the tape in men's individual — and Laura Lindemann grabbed gold for Germany in the mixed team relay event on Monday morning.

  1. GERMANY (Tim Hellwig / Lisa Tertsch / Lasse Lurs / Laura Lindemann)
  2. UNITED STATES (Seth Rider / Taylor Spivey / Morgan Pearson / Taylor Knibb)
  3. GREAT BRITAIN (Alex Yee / Georgia Taylor-Brown / Sam Dickins / Beth Potter) 

After claiming victory at the event's Olympic debut in Tokyo three years ago, Great Britain brought back half of its 2020 foursome — including Yee and Georgia Taylor-Brown, the latter of whom finished sixth in the women's individual — to clinch a second successive gold. But in a last-minute surge reminiscent of Yee's come-from-behind win less than a week ago, Laura Lindemann broke ahead of Great Britain's Beth Potter on the homestretch to thrust Germany atop the podium.
